# Context

The governed queue was empty for the right reason: every previously enabled continuation branch was retired, exhausted, or untracked.

Observed problem:

- there was no first-class artifact for proposing and approving a new family before it touched the queue

Chosen outcome:

- approve one materially new family with explicit rationale, not another local momentum tweak
- use `mean_reversion_broad_reentry` as the replacement thesis

Non-goals:

- automatic family invention
- multiple competing approvals in the same tranche
- broker or live-trading work
