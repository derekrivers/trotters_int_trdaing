# Risk Management

## Risk Stack

- Market risk
- Liquidity risk
- Model risk
- Operational risk
- Compliance risk

## Minimum Viable Controls

- Pre-trade: max order size and value, max daily loss, concentration and leverage caps, price collars.
- Live: drawdown circuit breakers, kill switch, message-rate monitoring, venue health checks.
- Post-trade: reconciliation, P&L attribution, incident logging, model drift checks.

## Data Integrity Blueprint

BCBS 239 is a useful template for accuracy, completeness, timeliness, lineage, and governance.
