# Strategy Templates

## Principle

Research should be treated as falsification: define hypotheses, invariants, failure modes, and evaluation criteria before running the strategy.

## Cross-Sectional Momentum

- Basis: documented momentum effect.
- Guardrails: liquidity filters, turnover constraints, transaction-cost model.

## Multi-Factor Value and Quality

- Basis: factor models such as size, value, profitability, and investment.
- Guardrails: sector neutrality and capacity constraints.

## Event-Driven

- Sources: RNS, NSM, and Companies House.
- Rule: only trade on information that can be shown to have been public at the time.
